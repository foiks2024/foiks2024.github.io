Maynard's

The Maynard is a beautifully refurbished 19-bedroom hotel with a bar and restaurant situated in Grindleford, nestled within the Peak District National Park. The Maynard's renowned restaurant offers a menu that showcases fresh, locally sourced ingredients. They cater to various dietary needs, including healthy options, plant-based dishes, and gluten-free selections. The Maynard sits amidst the picturesque Derbyshire countryside, making it the perfect base for exploring the Peak District National Park. For more information visit the website.
